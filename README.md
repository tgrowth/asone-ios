# AsOne - A Couple’s Guide to Harmony through Menstrual Cycle Tracking

AsOne is a thoughtfully designed app aimed at fostering understanding and reducing conflicts between couples by offering insights into the female partner’s menstrual cycle and its effects on mood, energy levels, and relationship dynamics. Through predictive tracking and personalized tips, the app helps couples navigate the unique rhythms of each cycle phase, enabling better communication and empathy.

## Features

### For Her

1. **Cycle Visualization**:
    - A circular calendar showcasing different phases of the cycle (Follicular, Ovulatory, Luteal, Menstrual) with distinct colors.
    - An optional linear timeline for an easy overview of the current cycle day.
2. **Daily Overview**:
    - Phase name and a brief description that includes mood and energy predictions.
    - Phase-specific advice on activities, wellness, and self-care.
3. **Freemium & Premium Advice**:
    - **Freemium**: General daily tips on mood, exercise, and self-care.
    - **Premium**: Tailored partner support tips for each day, guiding the partner on how to offer the best support.
4. **Calendar & Logging**:
    - A calendar highlighting key cycle dates, predicted periods, and ovulation.
    - Premium feature to log mood and symptoms for personalized tracking and insights.

### For Him

1. **Cycle Overview**:
    - A simplified, linear representation of the partner’s cycle with color-coded phases to easily identify where she is.
2. **Today’s Insights**:
    - Phase name and brief explanations tailored for a partner, offering insights on how she might feel during each phase.
3. **Support Actions**:
    - **Freemium**: Basic tips for supporting her based on the current phase.
    - **Premium**: Daily personalized support advice, including small actions and reminders for meaningful gestures.
4. **Notifications**:
    - Humorous and encouraging reminders on how hormonal shifts may impact her mood and energy, promoting better understanding and empathy.

### Shared Calendar & Partner Mode

1. **Calendar**:
    - Visual timeline of menstrual cycle predictions.
    - Premium hints on suitable activities and social plans based on each cycle phase.
2. **Partner Mode**:
    - Freemium tips for general relationship support.
    - Premium personalized insights, including mood logging and advice on communication, tailored to each partner’s preferences (love languages, activities).

### Love Languages Quiz

- A quiz for identifying each partner’s primary love languages, enhancing the customization of recommendations to better match personal preferences and relationship needs.

### Mood & Symptom Tracking (For Her)

- Logs moods, physical symptoms, cravings, and activities, offering insights into patterns over time.

## Phase-Specific Advice and Features

Each phase of the cycle is accompanied by suggestions and insights designed to foster understanding, respect, and connection:

- **Follicular Phase**: Energy rises. Tips for creative and adventurous activities.
- **Ovulatory Phase**: Highest energy. Ideas for social outings and romance.
- **Luteal Phase**: Slower pace. Encouragement for rest, support, and low-energy activities.
- **Menstrual Phase**: Lowest energy. Suggestions for relaxation, introspection, and minimal social activity.
